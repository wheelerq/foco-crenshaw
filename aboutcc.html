What We're About

Here at Crenshaw Crag we pride ourselves on being a climbing gym that's inclusive to all climbers. We provide bouldering and big wall climbing from V0 to V12, and even have a kids wall! Everyone is welcome here so we encourage you to come down, whether you're a beginner or ready to free solo El Capitan!

Memberships start at $40 per month
